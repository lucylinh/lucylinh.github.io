What
======
My friend Rita Steyn was having problems holding her phone in her underwater camera so I designed this insert with a cool pattern. This prevented her phone from over heating because its just plastic and it lined up her phone in the camera. 

Tools
======
I designed these parts in SolidWorks. 

Skills
======
I learned to use the linear pattern tool.
